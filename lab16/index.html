Ficus Lyrata

Thrives in bright indirect light. Can benefit from a few hours of full sun.Water every 1-2 weeks, allowing soil to dry out between waterings. Expect to water more often in brighter light and less often in lower light. Any humidity level will do. Normal room humidity is fine, although leaves may burn if the air is too dry.This plant will reach an ultimate height of about 30’ (10m) and will have a spread of about 10” (3.2m). However, as a houseplant, will be much smaller.Fiddles are happiest in a stable environment and should always be placed right in or near a window. Keep away from air conditioners, heaters and drafts.

Monstera Deliciosa

Thrives in bright to medium indirect light. Not suited for intense, direct sun but can be acclimated to withstand it.Water every 1-2 weeks, allowing soil to dry out between waterings. Expect to water more often in brighter light and less often in lower light. Monsteras can benefit from filtered water or leaving water out overnight before using. Normal room humidity will do, but prefers humid conditions if possible.It is an easy-going plant and is generally pest-free. Treat pests as soon as they appear with weekly sprays of horticultural (neem) oil and regular wipe-downs of the plant.
