~ Lab 21: Team Project Sketches Draft 2 ~

Designed by Haley Graham

Challenge

In this lab, we drew out wireframes for our final project and styled this html styled using Bootstap instead of our normal CSS styling. We also received feedback from our group on our first drafts of sketches before making these more detailed ones.

Problems

Because I tend to be a perfectionist, my sketches were not quick. I took my time, and in the end I have 3 very detailed wireframes my group members and myself can use to organize the pages of our final website.

Feedback and Results

My group liked how unique each of my designs were to one another. It is hard to get more feedback out of people when your designs are considered good or great. What I did to reflect the feedback of my peers was further develope my sketches. I used Adobe Illustrator instead of drawing them so I was able to mess around with fonts and colors as well as pay even more attention to detail. What you are seeing is an index.html, organized with Bootstrap and styled with the index.css that I wrote the code for.
